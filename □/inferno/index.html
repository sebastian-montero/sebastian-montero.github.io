🪦 star [2024]: The STyle And Recommendation (STAR) system was a computer vision-based application to detect a person's clothing styles based on their photos. This approach, coupled with personality questions and GPT-3.5 turbo allowed us to create personal style recommendations for the users. The app was developed completely in Python using Streamlit as its front-end for user testing. STAR was built as part of a working group exploring AI applied to fashion, however the group decided pursue to pursue part ways to pursue other personal goals.

🪦 luna writing [2023]: Luna was a one day hack building LLM-based writing assistant. This was an end-to-end tool for bloggers. Luna would help writers improve their writing along the creation and ideation phase. Once they finished their post, Luna would help them convert their content into twitter or instagram posts, summarise it or transform it into script for video creation. Future stages were planned to create trending topic features to inspire writers. Luna was build using Python And Streamlit as its front-end.
