Music

These are some of my favourite experiments in music composition and production.

My main gear is the Arturia KeyStep 37 Keyboard, but I sometimes also like using the PO-12 drum machine for drum loops.
On the software side I use Garageband and a (clandestine) version of Ableton Live. I also have Ableton Note when I am on the go but rarely use it.
All my tracks are currently hosted in Soundcloud.
