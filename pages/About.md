# sebastian montero paris
<br>
i am a senior machine learning engineer at [beamery](https://beamery.com/about-us/) working on deep learning models for recommendation systems.

projects at beamery:

- **talent match system**, built the first talent recommendation system for job matching using vector embeddings generated from our proprietary graph learning model.
- **talent entity inference system**, built a cross-entity recommendation system to surface similar hr entities based on one or several source entities (e.g. skills-skill, job-skill, industry-skill, etc).
- **skills inference model**, research and development of our skill inference model based on people's experience. the final version used a recurrent neural network feeding into a mixture of experts neural network architecture.  
- **candidate engagement model**, research and development of candiatate to recruiter engagement prediction models using tree-based methods.
- **talent tasks evaluation server**, built our ai evaluation suite to benchmark internal and external models across various talent recruitement tasks.

other previous work:

- **mckinsey & company's sila**, worked on mck's startup and investment landscape analytics solution to understand start up landscapes for global m&a. 
- **mckinsey & company's patent analytics** worked on mck's patent analytics solution to understand innovation areas across client's industries.
- **moodflix**, movie recommendation engine based on your mood using llms ([url](https://moodflix.streamlit.app)).
- **where2go**, event recommendations near you (_unreleased_).
- **lifelabs**, understand your wellbeing better by using llms to interface with your wearable data (_unreleased_).
- **misc oss contributions**, open source software contributions to _pyg-team/pytorch_geometric_ and _openai/chatgpt-retrieval-plugin_.
