=====
Music
=====
| These are some of my favourite experiments in music composition and production. 
|
| My main gear is the `Arturia KeyStep 37 Keyboard <https://www.arturia.com/products/hybrid-synths/keystep-37/overview>`_, but I sometimes also like using the `PO-12 drum machine <https://teenage.engineering/store/po-12/>`_ for drum loops. 
| On the software side I use `Garageband <https://www.apple.com/uk/mac/garageband/>`_ and a (clandestine) version of `Ableton Live <https://www.ableton.com/en/live/>`_. I also have `Ableton Note <https://www.ableton.com/en/note/>`_ when I am on the go but rarely use it.
| All my tracks are currently hosted in `Soundcloud <https://soundcloud.com/sebastianmxnt>`_.
