This is my first entry to my new website. I decided to update my website into what you currently see to start writing more often about anything that I find interesting. This could be books, movies, papers, art, food, music, etc etc etc. My idea is that this will be a personal outlet to transmit information and at the same time a way for me to preserve thoughts and ideas. This is my place to share raw unfiltered content.

I got inspired by the idea of a [digital garden](https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history). If you like this format I highly recommend this blog post.
